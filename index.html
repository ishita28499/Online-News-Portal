Importance

Facts and Figures

As online news portals have digitized the operations of many so-called “legacy” news organizations (those that originated in print or broadcast), this audience section presents data about digital-native news publishers – those originally founded on the internet.

Content Mapping

Content mapping is the first and most integral element of any successful news portal. As we all know, it is basically the process of making sure the right content is delivered to the right users, at the right time.

Hierarchy

A news portal consists of hundreds of stories, and so, it’s extremely important to determine which sections need to be highlighted, and which stories are of paramount importance to the readers.

User-friendly Navigation

Did you know that 40-60% of users don’t go past the homepage of a news portal when they visit one? The reason for this surprising finding is that most often, the navigation of news portals are quite mediocre in the Indian context, and isn’t user-friendly.
